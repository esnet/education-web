FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Lawrence Berkeley National Labratory? +

Located in Berkeley, California, LBNL is a leading research and development center for the U.S. Department of Energy. It is one of 17 national laboratories in the U.S. and one of the largest in the world. LBNL is home to 16 nobel peace prize winning acheivements and has discovered 16 elements as well.

What is LBNL's Address? +

1 Cyclotron Rd, Berkeley, CA 94720

Does Lawrence Labs have any educational oppurtunities for kids? +

Yes!! From summer programs & internships to school tours, shows and apps, LBL's K-12 STEM Education Outreach Program focuses a lot on providing oppurtunities for all kids. To learn more visit the K-12 Education Website!

Why are STEM careers important? +

STEM careers are imporant in leading innovation, making global discoveries and improving our lives. They are responsible for technological and medical advancements by solving problems and creating new solutions.
